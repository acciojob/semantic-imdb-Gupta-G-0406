Hera Pheri
Navigate to sections: Actors Quotes
Hera Pheri is a comedy film about three financially struggling men who get involved in a kidnapping ransom due to a series of misunderstandings, leading to hilarious situations.
The movie stars Akshay Kumar, Suniel Shetty, and Paresh Rawal, whose performances and comic timing made the film a cult classic.
One of the most famous quotes from the movie is: “Utha le re baba, utha le… mereko nahi re, in dono ko utha le!”
